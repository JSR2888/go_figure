About the creator

Hi, I'm Justin! I love math, engineering, sports, and word games! I'm studying mechanical engineering right now and I made this game as a side project. I hope you enjoy!

About the game

Go figure is based off of a game I learned from one of my elementary school teachers, using a deck of numbered cards. Once, when my classmates and I were stumped, he showed us that the only way to solve that particular puzzle was using a factorial. That was the first time I learned what a factorial is. Now, I hope this game teaches people new ideas and helps them think about math creatively. It's important to know that there's never just one way to solve a problem.
