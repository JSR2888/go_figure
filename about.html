About the game

Go figure is based off of a game I learned from one of my elementary school teachers, using a deck of numbered cards. Once, when my classmates and I were stumped, he showed us that the only way to solve that particular puzzle was using a factorial. That was the first time I ever learned about what a factorial is. Now, I hope this game teaches people new ideas and helps them think about math creatively.
